About Us

Ricardo Carneiro

Ricardo was in charge of the technical side of AdventureMate. He made sure the app was built on a strong and flexible foundation. Ricardo chose the right technologies for the project and made sure they fit the app's needs. He also took care of organizing and protecting the app's data.

Paulo Muniz

Paulo came up with the idea for AdventureMate. He worked on deciding what the app should achieve and how it should work for users. Paulo was in charge of leading the team, making sure everyone stayed on schedule, and he talked to important people involved in the project to get their thoughts and make sure the app developed the right way.

Shafqat Mashrakey

Shafqat was the design expert for AdventureMate. He made sure the app was easy to use and enjoyable for users. Shafqat created plans and models of how the app would look and work, tested it with users, and used their feedback to make it better. He also made sure the design followed good practices for inclusivity and accessibility.

Brianna Lopez

Brianna took care of all the paperwork for AdventureMate. She made sure every step of building the app was well-documented, and she worked on a detailed document called the SRS. Brianna also handled the testing part, making sure every feature of the app was thoroughly tested and matched what was planned. She talked with the team to keep everything on schedule and made sure all the tasks were done on time.
